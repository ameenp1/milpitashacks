## **INFORMATIONAL NOTICE ABOUT RECEIVING AUTOMATIC ELECTRONIC COMMUNICATIONS FROM THE COUNTY OF SANTA CLARA** 

When you provide your phone number and/or email address to the County of Santa Clara (“County”) Social Services Agency (“SSA”), you will automatically be subscribed to receive electronic communications (text messages, emails, and/or robocalls) from the County. 

The County will send electronic communications about things like future appointments and forms that you need to submit to keep receiving public assistance benefits. 

If your phone number and/or email address changes, you should let SSA know in a reasonable and timely manner so that you can continue to receive electronic communications. 

Receipt of electronic communications from the County may subject you to service provider charges or fees. The County shall have no obligation to pay such charges or fees. 

If you have any questions about this informational notice or if you want to stop receiving electronic communications, contact us:
